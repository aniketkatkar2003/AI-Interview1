Job Title: AI/ML Engineer (Fresher)
Location: [Pune / Onsite]
Employment Type: Full-Time
Experience Required: 0–1 Year (Freshers Welcome)

Job Summary:
We are seeking a passionate and self-motivated AI/ML Engineer to join our team. As a fresher, you will work alongside experienced professionals to build, train, and deploy machine learning models for real-world applications. This is an excellent opportunity for recent graduates who want to kickstart their career in AI and Machine Learning.

Key Responsibilities:
Assist in collecting, preprocessing, and analyzing structured and unstructured data.

Support in building and training machine learning and deep learning models.

Collaborate with data scientists and software developers to integrate models into applications.

Conduct model evaluation, tuning, and performance improvement.

Stay up-to-date with the latest research and development in AI/ML technologies.

Help in preparing technical documentation and reports.

Required Skills:
Solid understanding of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and AI fundamentals.

Proficiency in Python and familiarity with libraries like NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, or PyTorch.

Knowledge of data preprocessing, feature engineering, and model evaluation techniques.

Familiarity with version control systems like Git.

Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking skills.

Good communication and teamwork abilities.

Preferred Qualifications:
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, AI, or related fields.

Academic or personal projects in AI/ML.

Participation in hackathons, open-source contributions, or Kaggle competitions is a plus.
